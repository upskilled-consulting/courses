Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

  • Analytics — if you consent, we use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymized data about page views, session duration, and general geographic region. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • Progress data — quiz and lab progress is stored locally in your browser via localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

What we don't collect

  • No names, emails, or account information from course visitors.
  • No advertising or sale of data to third parties.
  • No tracking beyond Google Analytics.

Your choices

You can decline or withdraw analytics consent at any time by clicking Decline in the banner. You can clear your progress data by clearing localStorage for this site in your browser settings.
